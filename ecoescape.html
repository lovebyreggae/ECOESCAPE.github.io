Discover Maragondon

Maragondon offers for tourists interested in history, nature, and adventure. Maragondon is a Municipality in Cavite, Philippines, with a rich historical heritage. Maragondon is located on the eastern coast of Cavite.

Kaybiang Tunnel

The Kaybiang Tunnel is the longest underground highway tunnel in the Philippines. It spans approximately 300 meters and is located in the province of Cavite, connecting the towns of Ternate and Nasugbu. The tunnel was constructed to improve transportation and accessibility in the region, particularly for travelers going to and from popular tourist destinations in Batangas. It offers breathtaking views of the coastline and the West Philippine Sea, making it a favorite stop for visitors, especially motorcyclists, cyclists, and road trippers.

Maragondon Sanctuary

Maragondon Sanctuary is actually located at Ternate-Nasugbu Highway – adjacent to the shore of Sta. Mercedes and near the famed Kaybiang Tunnel. It is situated just less than a kilometer away from Patungan Beach, a weekend beach destination to Caviteños and other southies.
