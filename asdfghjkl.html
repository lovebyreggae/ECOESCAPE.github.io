KAYBIANG TUNNEL
The Kaybiang Tunnel is the Philippines' longest underground highway, stretching around 300 meters in Cavite province. It connects Ternate and Nasugbu, enhancing transportation to popular Batangas tourist spots. The tunnel offers stunning views of the coastline, attracting visitors, particularly motorcyclists, cyclists, and road trippers
